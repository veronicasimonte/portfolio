SUNNEE

SUNNEE, the beachwear brand that celebrates sustainability. For the graphic design project developed for start2impactUniversity, I created the logo, icons, and social media presentation. With the payoff "Let's dive into sustainability," SUNNEE highlights its use of recycled yarns for its products, combining style with environmental responsibility. The visual design reflects the brand's eco-friendly essence, inviting everyone to dive into a more sustainable future.
